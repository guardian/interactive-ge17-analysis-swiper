The Conservatives made significant gains throughout Scotland, especially in the southern cities

The largest gain was in Lincolnshire, where they gained 28 seats, 26 were from Labour

The Tories took control of Monmouthshire after 17 years of there being no party with overall control

The Conservatives lost 1 councillor in Doncaster, a Labour stronghold

They also lost 2 councillors in Flintshire, Wales, which has been under no overall control since 2012
